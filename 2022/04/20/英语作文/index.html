英语作文

阶段四书面表达

  • 假定你是李华,是某中学国际部的一名学生。校运动俱乐部将开设中国功夫(Chinese Kung Fu)课。请写一封邮件，邀请你校的英国交换生 Jim参加，要点如下:
  • 1.上课时间、地点;
  • 2.学中国功夫的好处;
  • 3.报名的截止时间。
  • 注意:写作词数应为80左右。
  • Dear Jim,
  • How’s everything going? I’m writing to invite you to attend a Chinese Kung Fu class to be held by our sports club.
  • Chinese Kung Fu enjoys great popularity in the world. Practicing Chinese Kung Fu can not only build up your strength, but also develop your character. You will become stronger and more confident. The class goes between 5:00 pm and 6:00 pm every Monday in our school gym next month. The closing date for entries is next Tuesday. Please let me know if you want to sign up for it.
  • Looking forward to your early reply.
  • Yours,
  • Li Hua
